# Safety Tools & Content Preferences

These preferences were established during Session Zero. They apply to every session
of this campaign. Any player or the GM may invoke these at any time — at the table,
before a session, or between sessions. No justification is ever required.

---

## Safety Tools in Use

- **X-Card** — Any player (or the GM) may tap the X-Card or say "X" at any moment
  to immediately skip, pause, or rewind the current scene. No questions asked.
  The scene either fast-forwards past the difficult content or is retconned away.

- **Lines & Veils** — Hard limits and soft limits documented below.

- **Open Door** — Any player may step away from the table at any time for any
  reason. The game pauses. No pressure, no explanation needed.

- **Check-In** — At the start of any session involving known sensitive territory,
  the GM will briefly check in with the table before proceeding.

---

## Lines — Include & Lean Into

These are the things this table actively *wants*. The GM should weave these in
with confidence.

| Theme | Notes |
|-------|-------|
| Investigations and mysteries | Layered puzzles, hidden truths, slow-burn reveals |
| Horror and suspense | Dread, atmosphere, the unknown — not gore |
| Secret guilds | Hidden agendas, veiled loyalties, rituals and ranks |
| Corrupt nobles | Political scheming, abuse of power, aristocratic rot |
| Forbidden magic | Dangerous knowledge, taboo rituals, things that reach back |
| Intelligent creatures | Monsters with culture, perspective, and reasons |
| Meaningful conflict | Violence that has weight, consequence, and purpose |
| Dragons | Presence, history, and power — ancient and contemporary |
| The Guild | An adventurers guild with a mission board as a campaign anchor |
| Inter-guild competition | Rivalry, sabotage, reputation, and professional pride |

---

## Veils — Hard Limits (Never Cross)

These topics do not appear in this campaign under any circumstances.
The X-Card stands as a backup, but these should never need it.

| Hard Limit | What This Means at the Table |
|------------|------------------------------|
| Gratuitous violence | Violence may exist with purpose and consequence; gore for its own sake, torture porn, or graphic suffering as spectacle does not appear |
| Sexual abuse | This topic does not appear in any form, implied or explicit |
| Loss of autonomy | Mind control, forced possession, and compelled action are not played as straight threats; if such magic exists narratively it will be handled abstractly, never dramatized against a PC |
| Bullying | NPCs do not demean, mock, or systematically humiliate player characters; inter-party cruelty of this kind is also off the table |

---

## Tone Agreement

Based on Session Zero discussion, the campaign tone is:

- **Genre feel:** Dark mystery / Gothic adventure / Political intrigue with wonder
- **Darkness level:** Grim with purpose — shadows are real, but so is the light
- **Violence:** Present, consequential, never gratuitous
- **Pacing:** Serialized with episodic beats — ongoing arcs with self-contained mission layers

---

## Revisiting These Preferences

Safety tools are a living document. Any player can request to add a new Line or
Veil at any time. The GM will update this file and confirm the change with the
full table before the next session.
