Five PCs established in session zero (2026-03-26). Running in Portuguese. Setting base: Parnamirim. Campaign title TBD.

**Characters:**

- **Sympla** — Rogue, Katari/Half-Elf. Mother was a political spy who was killed for being inconvenient. Father is an elven king (she knows; hasn't told anyone). Thrown out; raised by a kind old woman. Goals: gain king's trust AND revenge. Secret: knows her father's identity.

- **Alexander Von Derbilt** — Warrior, Simiah (half-monkey). Adopted by nobleman MAXIMILLION VON DERBILT (missing — left nothing behind). Uncle TALBUR is lying/suspect. Carries Maximillion's glasses (will break — plot point). From SOLAREN (may find people there have forgotten him and his father). Mantra: "Alexander sabe se controlar" — forged from a childhood fight where he lost control. Savage Ciph exploited this directly.

- **Llefys** — Ranger, Elf. Born with black skin; discarded by elven king. Raised by hunters ARVEN and MIRELA (killed by shadow spirits). Carries MOON COLLAR — last gift from Mirela ("A lua ainda te guia. Encontre seu caminho."). Called the Chosen of the Moon. DOES NOT enter elven territory.

- **Korapika** — Druid, Galapa (half-turtle). Master died protecting him. Master's DAUGHTER [name TBD] blames him. Discovered dark focal points draining energy from nature. Briefly touched one — felt something reach back.

- **Aroldo** — Bard, Ribbet (rat-folk). Raised by LORDE HITORI (underground court lord; Nijika's grandfather). Lost love NELLE stayed behind when band left. Inspired by NIJIKA (famous bard; Hitori's granddaughter; uses Pocket Drums magic instrument). Carries an ancient song (never performed; taught by Hitori's household). Savage Ciph erased him from group's memory in Session 2.

**Key thematic connection:** All five were abandoned, discarded, or forced to build their own place in the world. This is the emotional spine of the party.

**Critical plot synergy:** Llefys (shadow-spirits) and Korapika (dark focal points) are investigating the same supernatural corruption from different angles — almost certainly the main campaign threat's fingerprint.

**Potential twist (hold for right moment):** Sympla and Llefys may have been discarded by the same elven king — the same elven court. If confirmed, their shared wound becomes a shared story.

**Why:** Established by user in session zero and backstory session on 2026-03-26.

**How to apply:** Use these drives, wounds, and secrets to create spotlight moments, NPC encounters, and plot escalations tailored to each character. Alexander's glasses, Llefys's Moon Collar, and Aroldo's unperformed song are the three most load-bearing physical objects for emotional beats.
